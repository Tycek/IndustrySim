#IndustrySim
##Goal##
Turn based strategy sandbox single player game simulating industries, markets and AI competitors.
Build industries, produce materials or goods, sell them, profit, repeat.
##Features
- Industries that produce materials
- Industries, that accepts materials/products and produce different products
- Market
	- Companies and player can create offers and contracts
	- Offers are also generated by the game itself
	- One-time market offers (sell or buy)
	- Long-time contracts (sell or buy)
- Dynamic AI companies as competition
- Save/Load system
